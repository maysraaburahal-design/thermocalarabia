Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a thermal mapping study?

The duration depends on the facility size and regulatory requirements, typically ranging from a few days to two weeks.

Do you provide certified reports?

Yes, all our calibration and mapping reports are fully compliant and certified for local and international audits.

Do your services cover all regions of the Kingdom?

Absolutely. While we are based in Medina, our expert teams cover all regions of Saudi Arabia and the broader GCC.

What devices do you calibrate?

We calibrate a wide range of devices including temperature/humidity sensors, data loggers, pressure gauges, and lab equipment.

Do you supply storage condition monitoring devices?

Yes, supply is arranged after specification and agreement on requirements. A final QUOTATION is issued within one week to 15 days.
